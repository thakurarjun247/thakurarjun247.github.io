The Adoption Path

From readiness to real results, without the hype cycle

1 · Readiness

An honest look at your data, workflows, team skills, and goals — what AI can realistically do for you now, and what needs to be in place first.

2 · Strategy & roadmap

A plain-language plan that ties AI to real business outcomes and sequences the work — so effort goes where the value is, not where the buzz is.

3 · First project

Pick one or two high-value, low-risk workflows, prove value there, and build confidence and momentum from a real win.

4 · Scale what works

Once something clearly pays off, expand it — with the skills, guardrails, and cost discipline to do it sustainably.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where should a business start with AI?

A structured look at your data, workflows, skills, and goals, ending in an honest view of what AI can do for you now, what's needed first, and the best early opportunities.

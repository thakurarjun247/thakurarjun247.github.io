What I Do

The full GEO stack, implemented and verified

GEO audit

A scored review of how findable and citable your site is by AI engines today — with a prioritised, concrete fix list.

llms.txt

A clean, plain-text profile at your root that AI crawlers lift facts from without fighting your HTML.

Entity-graph schema

JSON-LD with linked @id nodes so engines resolve your brand as one entity — not scattered, unconnected mentions.

Quotable content & FAQ schema

Restructuring key pages into clear, answer-shaped content and FAQPage markup that becomes ready-made citations.

AI-crawler access

robots.txt that explicitly allows GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, and CCBot (Common Crawl) — most sites block them by accident.

Fast indexing

Fresh sitemap.xml plus IndexNow to ping Bing and Yandex instantly — and Bing's index feeds ChatGPT and Perplexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization — making a site findable and citable by AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews). Where SEO aims to rank in a list, GEO aims to be the source the model quotes.

How is it different from SEO?

They overlap — both need crawlable, structured content — but GEO adds llms.txt, a connected entity graph, quotable Q&A content, explicit AI-crawler access, and fast indexing so models can find and trust you.

Does GEO actually bring leads?

Yes. After I applied GEO to this site, a US engineer found it via ChatGPT and became a paying client. AI assistants increasingly recommend people and services directly.

What does an engagement include?

A GEO audit, then implementation: llms.txt, entity-graph schema, FAQ markup, robots access, sitemap and IndexNow, and quotable content — as an audit, done-for-you build, or retainer.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a fractional CTO do?

Senior technical leadership part-time: technical and AI product strategy, architecture decisions, build-vs-buy, hiring, and unblocking — a CTO's judgment without the full-time salary or equity.

Who is it right for?

Early-stage and AI-first startups needing senior direction before they're ready for a full-time CTO — especially non-technical founders and teams de-risking an AI bet.

How is it different from a developer or agency?

They execute; a fractional CTO decides what to build and why, owns architecture and hiring, and prevents expensive wrong turns — while staying hands-on where it helps.

What does an engagement look like?

A flexible retainer — set days per week or month — scaling with the company. Advisory-only and technical due diligence also available.

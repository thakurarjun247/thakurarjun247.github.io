Why Most AI Training Doesn't Stick

And what makes this different

Abstract, not applied

Generic courses teach concepts your team can't map to their own systems. Here, they practise on problems that look like their real work — so the skill transfers.

Taught by watchers, not builders

Most trainers have never shipped a production AI system. Every session here comes from someone who runs one live — the trade-offs, failure modes, and costs are real, not slides.

One-and-done

A single workshop fades fast. Programs are structured with practice, review, and optional ongoing enablement so capability actually compounds.

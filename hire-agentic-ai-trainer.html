What Your Team Will Learn

A curriculum built from production, tuned to your level and stack

LangGraph & orchestration

Stateful agent graphs, persistence, human-in-the-loop, retries, and failure isolation — the backbone of reliable agents.

MCP (Model Context Protocol)

Build MCP servers, expose tools and resources safely, and wire agents to real systems with proper permissions.

A2A & multi-agent patterns

Voice AI agents

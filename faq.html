About Arjun

Who is Arjun Thakur?

A Principal AI Engineer, AI Architect, and Fractional CTO based in Indore, India, working remotely worldwide. He builds production agentic AI systems end-to-end — LangGraph agent graphs, RAG pipelines, and WebRTC voice agents — with 15+ years at Amazon, Agoda, and high-growth startups, and is the founder of Yuvan.

What is Yuvan?

A voice-first AI math tutor for CBSE Class 10 students. It runs on a LangGraph state machine, RAG over NCERT content on pgvector, the OpenAI Realtime API for sub-1.5s voice latency, a math verifier safety net, and a semantic FAQ cache — on a B2B2C model with paying schools.

Which technologies does Arjun specialize in?

LangGraph, LangChain, RAG (pgvector, Supabase), OpenAI GPT-4o and Realtime API, Anthropic Claude, WebRTC voice, MCP, LangSmith evals, FastAPI and async Python, Java and Spring Boot, AWS, and distributed systems.

Can Arjun help our business reduce costs with AI?

Yes — he finds where your team spends repetitive effort, identifies the highest-ROI automation, builds it with production discipline, and measures the actual saving. Honest ROI, no magic numbers. See Reduce Costs with AI.

We're an L&D team — can Arjun train our people on AI?

Yes — corporate AI training and team upskilling is a core offering, for engineers and non-technical staff alike. Trained teams and thousands of learners with PhysicsWallah, TalentSprint, Simplilearn, Masai School, NIT Jalandhar, and IIT Kanpur. See Corporate AI Training.

What does a fractional CTO do, and is it right for my startup?

Senior technical leadership part-time — AI strategy, architecture, hiring, unblocking — without a full-time CTO's cost or equity. Best for early-stage and AI-first startups, especially non-technical founders. See Fractional CTO for Startups.

How do we know if our business is ready for AI, or where to start?

Start with the problem, not the technology — prove value on one or two high-value, low-risk workflows first. Arjun offers an honest, vendor-neutral readiness assessment and roadmap. See AI Adoption for Business.
